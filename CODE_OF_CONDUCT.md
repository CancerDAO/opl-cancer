# Code of Conduct

OPL for Cancer follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

The short version:

* Be kind. We're working on something that matters to real patients and their families.
* Disagree with the work, not with the person.
* No harassment, doxxing, sexual content, or political flame wars in this project's spaces.
* Patient data — even anonymised — is treated with the highest care.
* Maintainers may remove comments / commits / contributors that violate this code.

The full text is canonical. Read it at the link above.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, contact the maintainers at the address listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md). Reports are treated confidentially. For safety-relevant security or privacy issues, see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead.
